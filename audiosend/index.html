What You Get // Feature Overview
♪
AudioSend VST3 / AU
Installs in your DAW like any other plugin. On Windows it runs as VST3; on macOS as VST3 or AU. No separate app, no cloud account, no configuration required.
≈≈
Local Network Transfer
The plugin opens a local server when your DAW loads it. Scan the QR code once from your phone. From then on, every export appears on your phone automatically. Stream it, download it, done.
##
Built-in Audio Analysis
LUFS, true peak, dynamic range, stereo width, phase correlation, L/R RMS, DC offset, spectral balance — on the same page where you're listening. Real data, no extra tools.
