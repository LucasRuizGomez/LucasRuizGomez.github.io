What you get
🎚️
AudioSend VST3 / AU
Installs in your DAW like any other plugin. On Windows it runs as VST3, on macOS as VST3 or AU. No separate app, no cloud account, no configuration.
📡
How it works
The plugin opens a local server when your DAW loads it. It generates a QR code — scan it once from your phone's camera. From then on, every export appears on your phone automatically. Stream it, download it, done.
📊
Audio analysis, built in
LUFS, true peak, dynamic range, stereo width, phase correlation, L/R RMS, DC offset, spectral balance — on the same page where you're listening. Real data, no extra tools.

Every future plugin — included
The subscription is open. When I finish the next plugin, you get it automatically. Same price, no action required. Not a bundle — access to my work as it grows.
