How to activate
1
Download and run the installer for your OS. It places the plugin in your DAW's VST3 or AU folder automatically.
2
Open your DAW and load SongSender on any track or the master bus.
3
On first launch, the plugin shows an activation screen. Enter the email address you used to subscribe and your license key from the welcome email.
4
Done. The plugin verifies your subscription and unlocks. You won't be asked again on the same machine.
